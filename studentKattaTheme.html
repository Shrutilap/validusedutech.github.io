    Just a thought

    It is a vibrant platform for the Teenagers who are on the verge of becoming an adult for discovering their other aspects of personality . We welcome students from different domain into our STUDENTS' कट्टा's activities . We at STUDENTS' कट्टा consider studies as an extracurricular and your skills as curricular ! We build such community for students to interact on the basis of their skill set and not only studies . We emphasize on peer learning . Our motto is to develop a community which will have like minded people which surely provide an appropriate growth atmosphere.

    Why

    In today's time if you ask a teenager to introduce him/her , he / she often starts introducing themselves from their curricular achievements only. For future your curricular achievements will surely matter but what will also matter is your other skill sets . We at STUDENTS' कट्टा encourage our members to embrace their other skill sets as well ! It is to see yourself as a dynamic personality ! We define dynamic as a MULTIDIMENSIONAL personality . It’s only about personality development but to know about your own पैलू !

    Edges

    Members with different domains

    Meeting students from different domains will help oneself find application of his/her own skill set. It also helps to develop different skills ,which helps in your own understanding of your domain . You will start knowing the DYNAMICS of the world and where will you actually fit !

    Building meaningful connections

    Connections with people from different domains help in the long run . Some or the other member always need help / guidance from other domain's members to connect the dots between two different domains . This will help to get your problem solved . Having connections with like minded people helps you grow . Someone has said ; You will be defined by the people with whom you interact !
