    • गप्पागोष्टी

    • Venue : Waari Book Cafe, Kothrud Pune
    • Event Days : 2nd and 4th Saturday of every month
    • Timings : 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm
    • Duration : 20 minutes per topics
    • Contents : The word ‘discuss’ has been derived from the Latin root ‘discutere’, which means to shake or strike. Thus ‘discussion’ refers to thoroughly shaking up the subject, that is, examining it thoroughly to reach a conclusion.
    • The attendees will be presented with an impromptu topic. These will be topics from varying fields and suggestions from members are welcome. Current topics, science, hobbies, worldly matters, arts, music, finance, career, relationships etc..
  • Intention of this session :
    - is to actively listen and lend an ear to the presenter - take interest in the topic
    - is to raise curious questions and counter points that you feel appropriate to.
    - is to reason out your point of view in more than one ways so as to understand how a topic is perceived in your peer group
    - is to identify a solution to a common problem
