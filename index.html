    OVERVIEW

    Maths and Physics are not two DISTINCT subjects. They go hand-in-hand. This is the basic premise on which our fundamentals are built upon. Learn how to use mathematics as a language that precisely describes the physical world. Enhance your understanding by using tools like Geogebra, p5.js and Javascript to visualize math and physics. We at Validus EduTech put these seemingly separate topics together to create the immersive learning experience.

    ABOUT US

    Validus EduTech is a development center where we enroll students from science stream for physics and math. Utilizing our 17 years of expertise in teaching XI, XII, engineering subjects and we integrate mathematics into the study of physics. We go beyond the traditional approach of rote memorization and formula-based problem-solving and help you develop a deeper understanding of the concepts and their applications. Our philosophy dictates the premise that junior college math, physics, engineering math and programming skills should not be treated as disjoint sets, but as interrelated steps in your learning journey. We use a variety of TOOLS and techniques to make learning fun and engaging. Every student gets to explore, experiment, create and collaborate with his/her peers. Our study pipeline includes showcasing your programming skills on weekends.

    GOALS

    #1

    Foster creativity and innovation by encouraging students to explore new ideas and approaches in their engineering endeavors.                                                                  

    #2

    Nurture a multidisciplinary approach, enabling students to integrate knowledge from various fields and tackle complex engineering challenges.

    #3

    Develop proficiency in programming languages to effectively implement mathematical and physical concepts in real-world scenarios                                                        

    #4

    Foster a deep understanding of 11th and 12th-grade mathematics and physics as a foundation for engineering mathematics.
                                                           

    #5

    Encourage lifelong learning and a growth mindset by promoting continuous skill development and staying updated with emerging technologies.

    #6

    Promote teamwork and collaboration skills through group projects and interactive learning experiences.
